About

This is our senior Capstone Project. We are partnered with Alcon, also known as True Vision. Our goal was to create something that

could help surgeons, specifically eye surgeons do their job better

We did this by taking a post operation approach to the problem and used machine learing to solve the problem by providing surgeons with a post opertion highlight of the significant parts of the surgery.

To get started, click on home and upload a .mp4 file with surgery tools.
